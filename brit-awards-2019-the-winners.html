Brit Awards 2019: The Winners

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, March 8, 2024

The 39th Brit Awards took place in London tonight.

Hosted for the second year running by British comedian Jack Whitehall, big winners on the night included The 1975, Little Mix, Calvin Harris, Ed Sheeran and P!nk who was awarded the prestigious Outstanding Contribution To Music Award.

Check out the full list of winners below:

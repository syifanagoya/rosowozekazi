Will Durst Full Overview and Wiki

The Early Years and Rise

Born in the bustling city of Milwaukee, Wisconsin, Will Durst‘s early life was marked by a nomadic educational journey, attending 14 different schools before he found his calling.

The world of comedy welcomed Durst in 1974, with his debut at a Milwaukee bar called the Rusty Nail, setting the stage for a legendary career in political satire.

Durst’s educational path was unconventional; he hopped from one institution to another, including the University of Wisconsin and Marquette University, though he never donned a graduation cap.

Yet, this didn’t deter him. His real education came from the world, his experiences, and the people he met along the way.

A Career in Comedy and Political Satire

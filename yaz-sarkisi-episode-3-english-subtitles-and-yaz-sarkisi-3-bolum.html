Episode Summary

The last episode of "Yaz Şarkısı" left us with Yaz embarking on a new job, still unknowing that the singer Kemal, whom she holds a platonic love for, works there as well. With Asli's assistance, Yaz is hopeful that she can make Kemal fall in love with her. Meanwhile, Kemal is preparing for a song that he believes will catapult him to fame, but Yaz's plans take an unexpected turn, leading her to lose her job. Her mother, Emine, has high hopes for Yaz, who works at the bank.

Murat, the dependable savior, comes to Kemal's rescue yet again. However, he finds himself in a tight spot when Emine assumes that Yaz is furious after seeing Murat and Kemal together at home. Fadime's attempt to help Yaz only complicates matters further. Emine becomes convinced that Yaz and Murat are deeply in love, with Murat's marriage proposal lie seemingly becoming their reality. To add to the complexity, Emine believes that Yaz's employment at the company is all thanks to Murat's affection.

The Growing Lie

In the upcoming 3rd episode, the marriage proposal lie told by Yaz to her mother, Emine, begins to escalate beyond their control. With Emine's belief in their love and impending marriage, Yaz and Murat are left with no choice but to maintain the lie with utmost realism.

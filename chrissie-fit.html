Chrissie Fit is an American actress and singer who is known for playing the character Mercedes Juarez in the medical drama General Hospital and also for appearing as Florencia “Flo” Fuentes in Pitch Perfect 2 and Pitch Perfect 3. She has already established herself as a successful actress. Fit started singing at the age of 5 and discovered her inclination towards acting around the age of 10. Chrissie has a huge social media fan base with more than 1 million followers on Instagram and over 200k followers on Twitter.
